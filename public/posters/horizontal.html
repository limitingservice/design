HCI Master's Program • 2024

Research Assistant

Worked across two research labs focusing on interactive data visualization with museum field studies, AI-assisted qualitative analysis using Conceptual Metaphor Theory, and human-centered AI research with older adults.

Lab 1: Data Viz & Museums

  • → Goal: Evaluate interactive museum visual deployment.
  • → Methods: Deployed at Indiana State Museum; field studies using Kinect for movement tracking.
  • → Findings: Metaphors supported learning; Kinect effectively captured interaction.

Lab 1: AI & CMT

  • → Goal: Investigate LLMs for qualitative analysis using CMT.
  • → Methods: Scraped 5K+ Reddit posts. Refined prompts via Gemini and NotebookLM.
  • → Findings: High-confidence outputs aligned with experts; full consistency elusive.

Lab 2: AI for Older Adults

  • → Goal: Examine perceptions of AI systems for health info seeking.
  • → Methods: Prototyped AI artifacts. 20 usability testing sessions on trust/utility.
  • → Findings: Privacy highly concerning; transparency about limits increased trust.

Impact & Synthesis

  • ★ Supported real-world deployment of interactive installations.
  • ★ Demonstrated AI augments—but doesn't replace—human research.
  • ★ Confirmed embodied cognition principles apply physically and digitally.
